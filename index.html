Our Projects & Programs

Six active programmes turning environmental commitment into measurable impact.

Home / Projects

Reforestation

1. Tree Planting Campaign

A community-led reforestation effort restoring degraded farmland and watersheds through native tree species, seedling nurseries, and long-term forest stewardship training.

Ashanti Region Ongoing
Water & Sanitation

2. Clean Water for Communities

Drilling boreholes and installing filtration systems to provide safe, reliable drinking water while training local technicians in system maintenance.

Northern Region Ongoing
Waste Management

3. Plastic Recycling Program

Community collection points and a local processing hub that turn plastic waste into reusable materials, reducing pollution and creating green jobs.

Greater Accra Region Phase 1 Complete
Renewable Energy

4. Solar Energy for Villages

Installing affordable solar micro-grids to bring reliable electricity to off-grid communities, powering homes, schools, and health clinics.

Volta Region Ongoing
Climate Education

5. Climate Awareness Outreach

School and community workshops that build understanding of climate change causes, impacts, and practical local adaptation strategies.

Nationwide Ongoing
Community Development

6. Green Schools Initiative

Helping schools build gardens, waste-sorting systems, and eco-clubs that teach students sustainable habits from an early age.

Western Region 15 Schools Enrolled
