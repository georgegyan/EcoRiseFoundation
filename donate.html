Your Impact

Why Your Donation Matters

EcoRise Foundation relies on the generosity of individuals, businesses, and partners to run our reforestation, clean water, renewable energy, and recycling programmes. Every donation, large or small, goes directly toward materials, training, and community support on the ground.

We keep our operations lean and transparent, so your gift creates the maximum possible impact for the communities we serve.

What Your Gift Provides

The Impact of Donations

GHS 50 plants and maintains 10 tree seedlings for one growing season.
GHS 200 provides clean water access to one family for a full year.
GHS 500 contributes to a household solar power connection.
GHS 1,000 funds a full climate education workshop for a school.
